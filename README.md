# Bluez360

**Tagline:** See Inside. Build Trust. Gain Customers.

## Company Overview

Bluez360 creates Google-certified 360° virtual tours using Street View technology. We help businesses in Vadodara and the surrounding areas enhance their online presence with high-resolution, immersive tours that are compatible with any device. Our goal is to boost your credibility and attract new customers through Google Search and Maps.

The company is led by **Ajay Ajagiya**, a Google Trusted Photographer with over five years of dedicated experience in creating captivating virtual experiences.

## Customer Benefits

Choosing a virtual tour from Bluez360 offers several key advantages for your business:

*   **Enhanced Online Presence:** Become more visible on Google Maps and attract local customers actively searching for businesses like yours.
*   **Engage and Impress:** Offer a unique, immersive experience that captures attention and sets you apart from the competition.
*   **Boost Customer Confidence:** Allow potential customers to explore your space before they visit, building trust and transparency.
*   **Flexible Add-Ons:** Easily enhance your tour at any time by adding more photos, promotional videos, or other custom features.

## Services Offered

Our core service is the end-to-end creation and publication of professional 360° virtual tours.

*   **360° Virtual Tour Creation:** We handle the entire process, from professional photography to the final tour assembly.
*   **Google Maps Integration:** Your virtual tour will be published directly on Google Maps, linked to your Google Business Profile.
*   **Website Embedding:** We provide the necessary code to seamlessly integrate the virtual tour into your own website.
*   **Diverse Portfolio:** Our gallery showcases a wide range of projects, including:
    *   Interactive Virtual Tours
    *   360° Videos
    *   High-Resolution 360° Photos
