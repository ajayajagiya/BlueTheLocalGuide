About Us

Blue The Local Guide

We create Google-certified 360° virtual tours using Street View technology. We help businesses enhance their online presence with immersive, high-resolution tours that work on any device, boosting credibility and attracting new customers through Google Search and Maps.

I'm Ajay Ajagiya, the face behind Blue The Local Guide.

As a Google Trusted Photographer with over five years of in-depth experience, my mission is to virtually bring your customers to you. I create immersive 360° tours that allow anyone, whether they're local to Vadodara or halfway across India, to explore your business from their own screen.

My work bridges the gap between online discovery and real-world visits. By enabling potential customers to walk around your space before they even arrive, we build trust and create a connection that feels like being right there.
